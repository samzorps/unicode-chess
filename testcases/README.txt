The testcases can be ran by changing the value of the string "testcasefile" in Testing.java class.

castling.txt
- shows castling

movepawntwospaces.txt
- shows moving pawns two spaces 

foolsmate.txt
- shows check, checkmate
- showing capturing pieces
- shows moving pawns two spaces 

longtest.txt
- shows a long game of 20+ moves that goes into the endgame 
- shows castling on the kingside and queenside 
- shows moving pawns two spaces
